01 · Risks within Web3
Risks within Web3 security.

Understanding the unique security challenges facing DeFi protocols and Web3 applications.

Smart contract bugs.
Reentrancy, logic errors, unchecked calls can drain entire protocols. These vulnerabilities are permanent once deployed on-chain.
DeFi exploits.
Flash loan attacks combined with price oracle manipulation can drain liquidity pools within a single transaction.
Exchange breaches.
Hot wallet compromises and insider threats can expose millions in user funds across centralized exchanges.
Governance attacks.
DAO exploits due to weak voting logic can allow malicious actors to drain treasury funds or manipulate protocol parameters.
Regulatory scrutiny.
Need for compliance reporting and proof-of-reserves to maintain institutional trust and regulatory compliance.
