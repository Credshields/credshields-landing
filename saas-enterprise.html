Enterprise-Grade Protection for Cloud & SaaS Businesses

Modern SaaS and digital-first enterprises operate in multi-cloud environments, with complex infrastructures and millions of users.

CredShields provides penetration testing, cloud security reviews, and compliance-focused audits to help businesses scale securely and meet standards like SOC 2, ISO 27001, and GDPR.

Risks Within SaaS & Enterprise Security

  • Cloud Misconfigurations: Exposed S3 buckets or unsecured databases are among the top breach vectors.
  • Multi-Tenant Vulnerabilities: SaaS platforms risk cross-tenant data exposure without strict isolation.
  • Insider Abuse: Employee accounts or contractors misusing elevated access. Weak API Security: SaaS APIs often lack proper rate-limiting, enabling
  • Exploitation
  • Compliance Risks: Without SOC 2 / ISO audits, SaaS firms lose enterprise deals.

Why CredShields for Enterprises?

  • Expertise in cloud infra audits (AWS, Azure, GCP)

  • Data Privacy Breaches: Unauthorized data exposure leads to regulatory penalties under GDPR, CCPA, PCI DSS.
  • Tailored reports for boards, VCs, and enterprise clients
